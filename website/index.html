Everything you need to know about VirtuPet

VirtuPet syncs with Apple Health to track your daily steps. Your pet's health is determined by how close you get to your daily step goal. Walk more, and your pet thrives! You can also track activities, play minigames, and complete challenges.

Pet health = (Current Steps / Daily Goal) √ó 100. If your goal is 10,000 steps and you walk 6,700, your pet will be at 67% health. Your pet has 5 mood states: Sick (0-20%), Sad (21-40%), Content (41-60%), Happy (61-80%), and Full Health (81-100%).

Your pet's health resets at midnight local time. Each day is a fresh start‚Äîno punishment carried over from bad days! Daily achievements also reset if not completed.

Streaks track consecutive days where your pet ends the day at 100% health. This can be achieved by hitting your step goal or by playing pet activities. Break a streak by missing a day.

There are 5 pets: Dog (free), Cat, Bunny, Hamster, and Horse (premium). Each has unique moods and animations!

Free users get 5 credits daily, Premium users get 10 credits daily - all reset at midnight! Use 1 credit to play any minigame (+3 health) or do a pet activity like feeding (+5 health). All minigames are available to everyone. Need more credits? Buy extra packs anytime!

Complete various challenges to unlock achievements! Some are daily (like step goals), some are cumulative (like total steps walked), and some are one-time accomplishments. Track your progress in the Challenges tab.

VirtuPet automatically syncs when you grant HealthKit permissions during onboarding. Make sure you've allowed step count access in your device's Settings > Privacy > Health.

Yes! Go to Settings and tap 'Change Pet' to select a different companion. Premium members can access all 5 pets.
